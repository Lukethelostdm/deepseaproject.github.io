"Discovering the unknown, one dive at a time"

-Founder and CEO Miguel

Here at The Deep Sea Company, we strive to be the forerunners of exploration and oceanic knowledge. We strive daily to pass this knowledge onto those who want to learn either via our literature or through any of the numerous courses we have to offer.

We believe that there are many hidden secrets and fonts of knowledge at the bottom of the ocean. With our high end top of the line tech, we aim to prove this.

  • Trash Removed: 3.4 Million Tonnes
  • Current Projects: 12
  • Completed Projects: 71

Through years of hard work, we have established various routes for ecological improvement in our oceans. We believe that without a clean ocean, we cannot have a sustainable world. With this as our motivation we have set up numerous clean up events and sanctuaries across the globe. From Fiji and Thailand to Mexico and Ecuador, our message spreads across the globe.

Globally, the Deep Sea Company has helped to clear 1.4 million tons of garbage polluting our oceans. Our efforts have also helped the efforts of struggling marinelife, from seeing coral reefs grow and reclaim space to more hatchlings reaching the water. Through all of this, we have been showing people the wonders of the natural world and how amazing our oceans truly are, just below the surface.

From turtles to sharks to whales, the Deep Sea Company has been involved in all manner of conservation. Our state of the art tracking software allows us to gain new insight into the lives of these magnificent creatures. This research has led to new breakthroughs and better conservation efforts from multiple parties across the globe.
